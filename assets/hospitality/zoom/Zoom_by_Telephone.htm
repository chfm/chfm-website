Phone controls for participants

The following commands can be entered on your phone's dial pad while in a Zoom meeting:

  • *6 - Toggle mute/unmute
  • *9 - Raise hand

Notes:

  • Zoom meeting invitations designate which dial-in numbers are toll-free by adding "Toll Free" in parentheses after the number. If you do not see a parentheses after the dial-in number, then it is likely a toll number
  • If you decide to use a toll number to join a Zoom meeting, you will be charged based on the rates of your phone service provider.
  • Using a dial-in number for your country will be treated as a regular phone call. If you choose to use a dial-in number outside of your country it will be treated as a long distance call.
  • Unlimited local cell phone calls are generally free under most cell phone plans.
