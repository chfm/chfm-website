Name tag clips

These plastic clips are perhaps somewhat lighter than the metal ones. These were ordered from Amazon, 24 clips for $8.99.

24Pcs Plastic Badge Clips Badge Strap Clip with Clear PVC Straps for ID Card Name Tag Badge Holder

Name tag inserts

Rather than buy pre-cut inserts I have been buying white poster board from Dollar Store at $1 a sheet and cutting to size. This is cheap and more importantly the poster board is sufficiently thick and stiff to help the name tags stay upright in the wooden tray.

Wooden name tag trays

Our wooden trays came from Name Badge Productions. For some reason trays are relatively expensive and these are the cheapest.

Stick-on name tag labels

Most any will do but beware of labels without any border--they are hard to peel off because you can't see where the border is.
