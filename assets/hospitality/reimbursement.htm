To request a reimbursement:

  • Place an envelope containing a letter requesting reimbursement, signed by the requester and by clerk of Hospitality, into the "Finance" mailbox in the library.
  • Include in the envelope the original receipt(s) with the items purchased and total highlighted.
  • Email can substitute for letter (finance@chapelhillfriends.org) with scans of receipts attached, originals in envelope in Finance box.
  • A reimbursement check is placed in the Hospitality committee mailbox in the library. The treasurer usually sends an email when they have cut a check (so include your email in your letter).
