2. Suggestions for the Hospitality Committee
Here are the suggestion and decisions that were made. If you didn't attend,weigh in via email.

  1. Have a definite end time for committee meetings. We'll start as promptly after worship as we can and aim to end by 1:30
  2. Continue via email what we can't finish in a committee meeting.
  3. Jennifer volunteered to talk with Hart to discern how best to compile a list of people willing to be contacted to meet a visitor in person who requests such a meeting in response to our welcome card. Jennifer and Hart will tell us what they think is the best way to proceed.
  4. As for meal requests via Lotsa Helping Hands:
    • It is questionable whether there is any expectation on the part of C&C to come to Hospitality when there is a meal request (if fact they haven't done so in the 2 years since Lotsa Helping Hands was set up)
    • It is not the appropriate role of Hospitality to coordinate meal requests
    • However, our mission statement currently says, "coordinates Meeting response to requests for meals for Friends in need". So, we need to change our mission statement if we are to shed this role.
    • There is not a particular need for Hospitality to provide this function -- and there are probably better methods than Lotsa Helping Hands, e.g., "In the Light" emails.
    • So, we will place on the agenda for our next Hospitality meeting: (1) reconsider our mission statement, (2) consider reinstituting welcoming teas for recent visitors to the Meeting.
