Library Renovation:

There seems to be unity with the two committees on the following points (Lloyd and/or John may say a word about our joint efforts at the forum on renovation, Jan 26.

  • Fellowship after MFW should be in the library and not downstairs.
  • To facilitate serving coffee and tea during fellowship an enclosed tea/coffee cabinet with small bar sink and capacity to store cups and beverage supplies is desirable.
  • The island in the middle of the library should be removed to make space for the library, and a smaller (perhaps moveable) table be used for serving refreshments.
  • The carpet should be replaced with real or fake wood similar in appearance to that in the Meeting room.
  • The ceiling and lighting should be the similar to that in the Meeting room.
  • The accordion divider should be removed.
  • The bulky cabinet in the back that overlaps a bookcase could be replaced.
  • Should the fixed benches be replaced? Together with removing the free-standing cabinet, that would free up space for flexible comfortable and more user-friendly seating.
  • Given all the renovations needed to the library, would it make sense to get a preliminary architectural drawing to help envision how the space might better meet our needs now and in the future?

Thank you, Friends.
