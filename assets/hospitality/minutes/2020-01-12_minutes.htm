Library Renovation:

There seems to be unity with the two committees on the following points (Lloyd and/or John may say a word about our joint efforts at the forum on CHFM Renovations, Jan 26.

  • Fellowship after MFW should be in the library and not downstairs.
  • To facilitate serving coffee and tea during fellowship an enclosed tea/coffee cabinet with small bar sink and capacity to store cups and beverage supplies is desirable.
  • The island in the middle of the library should be removed to make space for the library, and a smaller (perhaps moveable) table be used for serving refreshments.
  • The carpet should be replaced with real or fake wood similar in appearance to that in the Meeting room.
  • The ceiling and lighting should be the similar to that in the Meeting room.
  • The accordion divider should be removed.
  • Given all the renovations needed to the library, a preliminary drawing of the library by an architect is desirable.
  • The fixed benches should be replaced with comfortable chairs to provide more flexible seating (this seemed to be the general feeling but not sure if there was unity)

Thank you, Friends.
