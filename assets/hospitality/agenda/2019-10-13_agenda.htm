2. Suggestions for the Hospitality Committee
At last month's clerks retreat, suggestions were solicited on how best to conduct committee meetings, and one particularly useful suggestion from John Hite was to ask committee members. So, what suggestions do you have for our committee? Here is what I might suggest:

  • Have a definite end time for committee meetings. I think I have put too much on each agenda with the consequence that meetings run too long. So, let's start as soon after announcements as you have had a chance to have some refreshment and return to the Meeting room. Then we'll focus on what agenda items we have time for and finish an hour after we start. How does that sound?
  • Continue online what we can't finish during a committee meeting. I know not everyone is enamored of computer interactions, but is there a better alternative?
  • Can one or two committee members discern the question of how best to compile a list of potential volunteers to respond to visitor requests to meet with a member of Meeting? And one or two committee members the question of how best to compile a list of potential volunteers to respond to meal requests? And then report back to the committee as a whole for consideration and approval?

Come with your suggestions.

Thank you, Friends.
