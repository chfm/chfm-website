2. What is Hospitality's role at memorial services?
An ad hoc committee of members from M&W, C&C, and CYRE has been working to put together a document of guidelines for celebrations at the Meeting for posting on our website. And they have asked for our input particularly with regard to our role at memorials. Let's discuss with the goal of putting together a short statement of our role for inclusion in that document.

3.Planning for the coming year
What topics require discernment? Clearly we can't plan the year in advance, but I'd like to set the stage. Following are some questions that I have in mind to discuss during the year.

  • Lotsa Helping Hands: Two years ago we asked Friends to sign up who were willing to be notified when a Friend needed help with meals. Ten people signed up. Should we ask again?
  • Refreshments: What should we provide? How are we reimbursed?
  • Visitors: Should we compile a list of members who are willing to be notified when a visitor would like to be contacted?
  • Visitors: In the past, Hospitality has hosted a tea for recent visitors to the Meeting. Should we do this again?
  • Thanksgiving: Asking for volunteers to host someone at Thanksgiving hasn't worked well the last few years. Should we lay it down or is there something we can do?

What other topics would you like to see addressed?
